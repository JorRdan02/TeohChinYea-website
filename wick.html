MODULE 1: Kiosk Machine Animation with Wick Editor

PThis module employs the Wick Editor to create an engaging kiosk machine animation, providing users with an interactive and visually captivating experience. Step 1: User Interaction The animation begins with a user-friendly interface prompting users to click, initiating the interactive experience. Upon clicking, users are prompted to select their preferred photo from a visually appealing array of options. This initial step sets the tone for an immersive and participatory animation, where users actively engage with the kiosk machine interface. The integration of the Wick Editor ensures a seamless and dynamic user interaction, enhancing the overall appeal of the kiosk machine animation.

MODULE 2: Interactive Frame Selection

Step 2: Frame Selection After choosing a preferred photo in Module 1, users are seamlessly guided to a frame selection interface. Here, they can peruse an assortment of frames to complement their chosen image. Importantly, users have the flexibility to make adjustments and, if needed, backtrack to correct any accidental selections. This user-friendly functionality adds a layer of personalization to the animation, allowing individuals to curate their visual experience. The inclusion of a backtrack option reflects a commitment to a smooth and forgiving user experience, ensuring satisfaction and enjoyment throughout the interactive frame selection process. The use of the Wick Editor continues to contribute to the fluidity and responsiveness of this engaging animation module.

MODULE 3: Photo Confirmation and Printing

Step 3: Photo Confirmation and Printing Upon completing the frame selection in Module 2, users are directed to a dedicated page where they can review their chosen photo and frame composition. A user-friendly interface provides options for confirmation or revision. Users have the flexibility to make any last-minute adjustments or proceed with confidence. Upon confirmation, the animation seamlessly transitions to the printing phase, symbolizing the culmination of the user's interactive journey. The selected photo, adorned with the chosen frame, is printed and presented to the user as a tangible memento, capturing the essence of the famous Penang photo experience. This module encapsulates the user's engagement in a visually delightful and memorable manner, emphasizing the intersection of technology, user interaction, and the creation of a personalized physical keepsake. The integration of the Wick Editor ensures a cohesive and enjoyable animation experience from start to finish.
