MODULE 1: Diwali SEGI Celebration Landing Page

MODULE 1: Diwali Celebration Landing Page This landing page serves as an exclusive gateway to the SEGI Diwali Event, offering a user-centric experience focused on providing information about the upcoming festivities. The main page is thoughtfully designed to allow users seamless access to details about the upcoming Diwali event. Immerse yourself in the vibrancy of Diwali with our visually appealing landing page, where users can effortlessly explore and anticipate the upcoming event. This user-friendly interface ensures a captivating and informative experience, reflecting SEGI's commitment to celebrating cultural events with a touch of elegance and accessibility.

MODULE 2: Comprehensive Course Catalog Integration

In Module 2, the landing page expands its functionality to provide users with a holistic view of the courses offered during the Diwali event. Users can seamlessly navigate through a dedicated section, where a diverse range of courses is showcased, including IT, Hotel Management, Cybersecurity, and more. This integrated approach ensures that visitors can not only stay informed about the upcoming Diwali event but also explore the array of educational opportunities available. The user-friendly interface exemplifies SEGI's commitment to facilitating easy navigation and access to relevant information, fostering an engaging experience for individuals interested in both cultural celebrations and educational pursuits.

MODULE 3: Course Details and Participant Overview

In Module 3, the landing page elevates the user experience by providing in-depth details about each course, along with a comprehensive overview of enrolled participants. Users can delve into specific course information, such as curriculum, instructors, and schedule details. Furthermore, a dedicated section showcases the list of students who have enrolled in each course. This module not only enhances transparency but also encourages potential participants to make informed decisions about their course selections. The incorporation of participant details adds a layer of community engagement, fostering a sense of connection among students. SEGI's commitment to providing a transparent and inclusive educational experience is reflected in this module, where information about courses and student participation seamlessly converge.
